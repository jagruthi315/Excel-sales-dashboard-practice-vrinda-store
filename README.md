
# 📊 Vrinda Store Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel -practice)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the annual sales performance of **Vrinda Store** for the year **2022** using **Microsoft Excel**. The objective was to explore customer purchasing behavior, identify key sales trends, and generate business insights through interactive dashboarding.

The dashboard was built as part of my Excel learning journey to strengthen my skills in data analysis and business storytelling.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Vrinda Store wanted to understand its sales performance during 2022 and identify opportunities to improve revenue by analyzing customer demographics, product categories, sales channels, and regional performance.

---

# ❓Business Questions

This dashboard answers the following business questions:

- How do monthly sales compare with the number of orders?
- Which month recorded the highest sales and order volume?
- Who purchased more in 2022: Men or Women?
- What are the different order statuses?
- Which states contributed the highest sales?
- How does purchasing behavior vary across different age groups and genders?
- Which sales channel generated the highest revenue?
- Which product category sold the most?

# 🔍 Key Insights

### 📈 Sales Performance

- March recorded the highest sales as well as the highest number of orders, indicating a peak shopping period during the year.
- Monthly sales and order quantity showed a positive relationship, meaning higher order volumes generally resulted in higher revenue.

### 👩 Customer Demographics

- Women contributed approximately **64%** of the total purchases, making them the primary customer segment.
- Adult customers generated significantly more sales than Teen and Senior customers.
- Across all age groups, women purchased more products than men.

### 🌎 Regional Performance

- Uttar Pradesh emerged as the highest revenue-generating state.
- Maharashtra, Haryana, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Rajasthan and Andhra Pradesh also contributed significantly to overall sales.

### 🛍 Product & Sales Channels

- Amazon was the highest-performing sales channel, contributing the largest share of total sales.
- "Set" was the best-selling product category, followed by Kurta and Western Dress.

### 📦 Order Status

- Approximately **92%** of all orders were successfully delivered.
- Only a small percentage of orders were cancelled, returned, or refunded, indicating a relatively healthy order fulfillment process.

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can help improve business performance:

### 1. Focus on High-Value Customers

Launch targeted marketing campaigns, loyalty programs, and personalized offers for **adult women (30–49 years)**, as they represent the largest customer segment.

### 2. Strengthen Top Sales Channels

Since Amazon contributes the highest revenue, ensure sufficient inventory availability and prioritize promotional campaigns on this platform.

### 3. Optimize Inventory

Maintain adequate stock levels for the highest-selling product categories such as **Sets, Kurtas, and Western Dresses** to reduce stockouts and maximize sales.

### 4. Improve Seasonal Strategy

Sales declined during November and December compared to earlier months. Further analysis should be conducted to determine whether the decline was caused by seasonality, inventory shortages, pricing strategies, or changing customer demand.

### 5. Expand Regional Marketing

Develop region-specific marketing campaigns and festive offers for both high-performing and underperforming states to increase customer engagement and revenue.

### 6. Understand Customer Preferences

Collect customer feedback through surveys or post-purchase questionnaires to better understand purchasing behavior and identify opportunities for improving customer experience.
